Processo

Crie sua primeira página web com Bootstrap

  1. No repositório "portifolio-info1-2026":
    • Crie uma pasta dentro da pasta "html" chamada "bootstrap".
    • Crie um arquivo na pasta "html/boostrap" chamado "intro_boostrap.html".
    • Adicione a estrutura básica de html, utilizando o atalho "html:5" ou "!".
  2. Adicione a seguinte <meta>tag dentro do <head>elemento:
    • <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
  3. Incluir no elemento <head>::

    • <link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.3.8/dist/css/bootstrap.min.css" rel="stylesheet" integrity="sha384-sRIl4kxILFvY47J16cr9ZwB07vP4J8+LH7qKQnuqkuIAvNWLzeN8tE5YBujZqJLB" crossorigin="anonymous">

    • <script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.3.8/dist/js/bootstrap.bundle.min.js" integrity="sha384-FKyoEForCGlyvwx9Hj09JcYn3nv7wiPVlz7YYwJrWVcXK/BmnVDxM+D2scQbITxI" crossorigin="anonymous"></script>

  4. No corpo do arquivo "intro_boostrap.html" inclua os paragrafos:
    • <p class="text-primary">.text-primary</p>
    • <p class="text-secondary">.text-secondary</p>
    • <p class="text-success">.text-success</p>
    • <p class="text-danger">.text-danger</p>
    • <p class="text-warning">.text-warning</p>
    • <p class="text-info">.text-info</p>
    • <p class="text-light bg-dark">.text-light</p>
    • <p class="text-dark">.text-dark</p>
    • <p class="text-muted">.text-muted</p>
    • <p class="text-white bg-dark">.text-white</p>
